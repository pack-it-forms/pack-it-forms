How to use EOC Form 213RR
Purpose To request non-mutual aid supplies, services, personnel, teams, equipment, utilities, fuel, facilities, or any other resource or incident management activity required from the Operational Area (OA.)
When to use Any time during any Operational Period. If the OA EOC is not activated the Duty Officer will serve to coordinate the request.
Prepared by Any EOC position or agency requesting resources from the OA EOC.
Approved by Section Chief of the requesting EOC or Supervising Official at Requesting Agency
Routed to Planning Section Resource Status Unit -> Logistics Section -> Finance/Admin Section -> EOC Director -> Planning -> Intelligence Section
Filed with Planning Section Documentation Unit.
User Notes This is a two-sided form. Side one is completed by the requestor. Side two is completed by the OA EOC. Please check that both sides are available.
